Aebibtech is a site that contains guides regarding computers and networks that can be useful for individuals and business.

Story

I started learning about computers since 2006. The PC technician that was servicing our business (having at least 12 computers at that time) way back 2009 was implementing solutions that does not last long. Those computers were being taken down by viruses every month. I realized that I needed to learn more about it. I started servicing other people’s computers when I finally got a grasp on how computers work and how to maintain computers. I started Aebibtech to help people regarding computers. Computing is supposed to make our lives easier. One can only harness the power of computing by knowing more about it.
